# Ad Format & Brand Guidelines (Sample)

This document contains reference guidelines for advertising scripts. When generating testcases, ensure you verify that these constraints are met if applicable to the ad format.

## General Video Requirements
- **Duration**: Usually 15s, 30s, or 60s. Ensure the script timeline aligns with the target duration.
- **Aspect Ratio**: 16:9 for YouTube/TVC, 9:16 for TikTok/Reels/Shorts, 1:1 for Facebook/Instagram Feed.

## Brand Guidelines
- **Logo Placement**: Must always be visible in the top-right or bottom-right corner.
- **Brand Colors**: Primary colors must be used for text highlights or Call-To-Action (CTA) buttons.
- **Fonts**: Use the official brand font (e.g., Roboto or Montserrat) for all on-screen text.

## Audio & Voice Over
- **Background Music (BGM)**: Must fade out slightly during Voice Over (audio ducking).
- **Tone**: The Voice Over should be energetic and clear.

## Compliance
- **Disclaimers**: Any claims like "Number 1" or "100% natural" must have a visible on-screen disclaimer (usually at the bottom of the screen, small font) for at least 3 seconds.
- **Call to Action (CTA)**: Every ad must end with a clear CTA (e.g., "Download Now", "Learn More", or "Buy Today").

*(Note: Users can edit this file to include their specific project or company guidelines.)*
